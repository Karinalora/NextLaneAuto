Why Should You Choose Us?

At NextLane, we pride ourselves on a customer-first approach and we strive for customer satisfaction. At the end of the day, we would not be here without you. It is true, we are not like a traditional car dealership, but what sets us apart is that:
1
We are understanding. We know you might be working, have kids, families, friends, things to do. We want to make this whole process stress-free and convenient, so we do all the legwork for you. We will find the perfect vehicle that fits your needs, handle the negotiation process, and deliver it to you at the comfort of your very home.
2
Unlike a standard dealership where you’re just a number, we strive for a personalized relationship with you and offer 1-1 support to tailor your specific needs.
3
We are car-lovers and we love caring about you. We want to make your purchase experience as smooth and enjoyable as possible. We will ensure that you have a car that meets your needs, handles the negotiation process, and delivers it to you at the comfort of your very home.
