At NextLane, our story begins in the hallways of Hillcrest High School, where three friends bonded over a shared passion for cars. From day one, we were the trio who spent their free time in the library talking about car designs, about owning different cars, and dreaming of the open road ahead. What started as conversations in the library grew into a lifelong commitment to the automotive world.

Over the years, our love for cars evolved into a vision: to create a service that not only connects people to their dream vehicles, but also gives back to the community that shaped us. With this goal in mind, we founded NextLane, an auto leasing company that focuses on simplifying the process of getting behind the wheel of a car you love.

But, more than just leasing cars, we’re about building relationships. Growing up, we learned the value of community and we aim to bring that same ideology to our business. Whether you’re looking for a family-friendly SUV or a sleek sports car, we’re here to help you find the perfect fit and onto the Next Lane.
